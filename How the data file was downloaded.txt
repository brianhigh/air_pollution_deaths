Download IHME GBD (2023) data [Air pollution: Particulate matter] Deaths (%)
  - Load https://vizhub.healthdata.org/gbd-compare/ in your web browser.
  - In left side configuration area:
      - Select: Single, [plot type icon] = [Risk by Cause],
            Category = All risk factors
            Level = 1
            Measure = Deaths
            Location = Global
            Year = 2023
            Age = All
            Sex = Both
            Metric = %
  - In center plot area: 
      - Click "Environmental", "Air pollution", "Particulate matter"
  - In upper right (to the right of "GBD Compare" page title):
      - Click "Download" -> "Chart data (csv)"
Do this manually, as a login/password is required and difficult to automate.
